How to share screen with audio in Zoom on Desktop/Android/iOS

Desktop

  1. Click on the green “Share Screen” button.

  2. Pick the window you want to share

  3. Check the “Share computer sound” box so the participants can hear the video

  4. If you are sharing a video and not a PowerPoint, check the “Optimize Screen Sharing for Video Clip” box as well.

  5. Click “Share”

  6. When you’re done, hover your mouse at the top of the screen and click the small “Stop Share” button that appears.

Android

  1. Click on the green “Share” button

  2. Scroll down and click on “Screen”

  3. A warning will pop up to remind you that everything on your screen will get shared. Click the “Start Now” option.

  4. If it requests permission to display over other apps, click on the slider to allow it. Then go back to zoom.

  5. Click the small arrow that appears on the left-hand side of your phone.

  6. Click the button that says “Share Audio: OFF”

  7. When you’re done with the video, click the arrow on the left-hand side of the screen and click the red “Stop Share” button.

iOS

  1. Click on the green “Share Content” button

  2. Click on “Screen”

  3. Scroll down and click on “Zoom”

  4. If the right side says “Microphone Off”, then click it to turn it on.

  5. Click “Start Broadcast”

  6. Go back into zoom and check that the top button is set to “Share Device Audio: On”

  7. When you’re done, go back into zoom and click the the red “Stop Share” button.
