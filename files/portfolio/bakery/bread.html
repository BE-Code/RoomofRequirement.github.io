        We love baking fresh whole wheat products for you. This month we're so excited to introduce you to Sonoma Bread! It is 100% whole grain and 100% delicious. Golden raisins, cranberries, and lemon and orange zest give it a bright and refreshing taste. Pumpkin and sunflower seeds add texture, a nutty flavor and crunch. There are many ways to enjoy Sonoma Bread. We slice it thin, toast it and serve it with an herbed goat cheese, fresh fruit and our favorite wine. It is divine!
